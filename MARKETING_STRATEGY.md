- **Key Tagline**: *"Your House, Your Command. Anywhere, Anytime."*

- **Launch Phase**: Send exclusive "First 50 Customers" discount invites to build a sense of urgency and exclusivity.
- **Personalization**: Use a sophisticated, concierge-style tone in all communications to maintain the premium brand image.

## 🔗 focus-control-3 Integration
